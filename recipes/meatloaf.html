Meatloaf

Description

This recipe is perfect if, like my husband, you can't stand ketchup smothered over the top of your perfectly good meatloaf. Instead, this will give you a perfectly juicy meatloaf with a delicious brown gravy to put over the top.

This recipe is a family favorite, with everyone from the youngest to the oldest enjoying it.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onions
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tbs chopped garlic
  • 1 tbs fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 tbs Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs, beaten
