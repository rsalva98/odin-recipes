Tater Tot Casserole

Description

This recipe is not only quick and easy, but it's a fan favorite that everyone can enjoy. It's simple, tasty, and requires very little effort to feed a whole family.

As a mother, I love the ease with which I can whip this up. It means less time on my feet in the kitchen and more time enjoying dinner with my family.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 (16 oz) package frozen tater tots

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until no longer pink and completely browned, about 7-10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Stir cream of mushroom soup into the cooked ground beef. Pour the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer tater tots evenly over the ground beef mixture and top with cheddar cheese.
  4. Bake until tater tots are golden brown and hot, about 30-45 minutes.
